About

The Rivers State University Security Incident Reporting (RSUSIR) is a web-base reporting platform that is in responsible for monitoring, gathering, analizing and mitigation of all security threats and incident whether intentional or non intentional happening only within the University campus. Its aim is to minimize, if possible eliminate the rise of security threats. Its also provides students, staffs and visitors to the University a medium where they can report incident anonimously without the fear of being repremanded.

As a University, we strive to keep our word of being Excellent and creative in all our endeavours and to protect our campus from internal and external attacks.
